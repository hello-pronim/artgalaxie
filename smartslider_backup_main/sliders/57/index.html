Personal branding is the process of developing a "mark" that is created around your name or your career.
We all can be a brand and cultivate our power to stand out and be unique. This uniqueness draws people to our product, our services, or even just our message. Your personal brand should be about who you are and what you have to offer. Personal brands are not only for the entrepreneur that owns their own business. It is the secret sauce that can make you stand out of a stack of resumes. Developing your personal brand is the proactive way of controlling your career development and how you are perceived in the marketplace.
